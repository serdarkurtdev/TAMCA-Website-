News and Updates at TAMCA

Our Services

Education

We offer in-depth courses for our children to discover and learn about their roots. We also provide seminars and speakers for adults who want to remain connected to their community.

Community

We have multiple locations that act as a hub for our community to get together, meet each other, and establish more connections

Support

We offer support in many forms, whether it be spiritual, financial, or resourceful for those both new and tenured.

Memorial

We understand it can be tough losing a loved one, we also understand the many steps it takes to go through the entire process. We provide immediate support and relief for Memorials and funerals.
